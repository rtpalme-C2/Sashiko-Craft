Your App

Open your inventory from any device — phone, tablet, or computer — at:

https://rtpalme-c2.github.io/Sashiko-Craft/sashiko-craft-inventory.html

No Terminal, no local server needed. Just open the URL in Chrome, sign into Google Drive when prompted, and you're in.

Bookmark this URL on your phone so it's always one tap away.
Scenario: You just bought new products

Step 1 — Open the app

1
Open the URL above in Chrome on any device.
2
Click Connect Google Drive and sign in if prompted.
3
Wait for the dot to turn green — you're connected and your current data is loaded.

Step 2 — Add each new item

1
Click + Add Item in the top right.
2
Fill in the fields. At minimum, enter the Name, Category, Qty, and Status.
3
Click Save Item. The item appears immediately and syncs to Google Drive within a few seconds.
4
Repeat for each new product.
Which status to use
Not Yet Ordered En Route Delivered
Just placed the order → En Route
Planning to buy but haven't yet → Not Yet Ordered
Already in hand → Delivered

Scenario: Your order arrives
1
Open the app and connect Google Drive.
2
Click the En Route status filter to see only items in transit.
3
Find the item that arrived. Click Edit.
4
Change Status to Delivered. Add a delivery date to Notes if you like, e.g. Delivered Mar 28.
5
Click Save Item. Done — syncs automatically.
Scenario: You're out shopping and want to check your inventory

Open the URL on your phone in Chrome. Connect Google Drive. Your full inventory loads — you can search, filter by category, and check what you already have before buying duplicates.

The app works on any browser on any device. No app to install, no account to create beyond Google.
Scenario: You have an invoice and want Claude to update the inventory

If you receive an order confirmation or invoice and want help adding multiple items at once:

1
Come to claude.ai in this project.
2
Upload the invoice or paste the order details and say "Add these to my Sashiko Craft inventory."
3
Claude will update the sashiko-craft-inventory.html file and give you a download link.
4
Upload the updated file to GitHub to replace the old one:
Go to github.com/rtpalme-C2/Sashiko-Craft → click the file → click the pencil/upload icon → upload the new file → click Commit changes.
5
Wait about 1 minute for GitHub Pages to update, then open the app — your new items will be there.
This is the only scenario that requires a GitHub upload step. For individual adds and edits, just use the app directly.
